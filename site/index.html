How it works

1 · Paste a searchFilter on yad2 exactly how you like — model, price, rooms, area — and drop the URL into your config.
2 · It watchesEvery 30 minutes it checks for new listings and pulls the real details (km & test for cars; ₪/m², floor & condition for apartments).
3 · AI triagesAn LLM scores each listing 0–10 against what makes a good deal, with a one-line reason. Low scores are hidden.
4 · You get pingedThe keepers land in your Telegram — solo or a shared group — the minute they're posted. Be first to call.

Why it's different from yad2's own alerts

🤖 AI deal-scoring

Not just "new listing" — a 0–10 score and a reason, so junk and overpriced ads never waste your time.

⚡ Real-time

Minutes, not a batched daily email. In a fast rental market, first-to-call wins.

🏠🚗 Multi-vertical

Cars and rental apartments today; any yad2 category with a tiny adapter.

👫 Share with anyone

Point it at a Telegram group so you and your partner hunt together.

🔒 Yours alone

Self-hosted. Your searches, your machine, your data. No third party in the middle.

🧩 Open source

MIT licensed. Read it, fork it, change the scoring rubric to your taste.

Install in 5 minutes

git clone https://github.com/<you>/yad2-watcher
cd yad2-watcher
cp config.example.json config.json   # add your Telegram bot token + searches
python3 watcher.py --setup-telegram  # link your chat
python3 watcher.py --now             # first run
⚠️ Personal-use tool. You run it yourself, on your own connection, against your own saved searches — it doesn't redistribute yad2's data. yad2's Terms of Use restrict automated access; understand them before running, keep the volume gentle, and stop if asked. No warranty.
